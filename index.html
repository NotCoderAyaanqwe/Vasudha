Electricity Generating Speed Breakers

Our Vision

The purpose of this project is to explore and develop an innovative solution for sustainable energy generation by transforming a common traffic management tool, the speed breaker, into a source of renewable electricity. In light of the growing demand for clean energy and the need for efficient use of existing infrastructure

Our Mission

We hypothesize that integrating a mechanism within a speed breaker that captures and converts the kinetic energy from vehicles into electrical energy is feasible and can be done efficiently. Specifically, we propose that:

1. Energy Capture: The kinetic energy generated by vehicles passing over a speed breaker can be harnessed using a streppor motor and a DC generator. When vehicles drive over the speed breaker, the mechanical energy

2. Storage and Utilization: Motion will activate the streppor motor and the DC, generating an electrical current.

3.Cost-effectiveness of the project and practical utility.

Our Product

Daily use:- Electricity will be generated and stored When the vehicles pass through the speed breaker. This electricity could be used as normal electric energy for various purposes like street lamps, houses, factories, etc. .

Installation:- These speed breakers will be very easy to install. The would be installed just like a normal speed breaker but it will have more wires and circuits. Through underground wires it will transfer electricity.
